1. Introduction

Tuticorin Container Brokers Association (TCBA) operates the TCBA Mobile Application to facilitate container movement processing, stakeholder approvals, wallet management, and secure digital payment transactions between registered port stakeholders.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information. The application is intended strictly for registered business stakeholders.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Name
  • Organization details
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Stakeholder type

2.2 Financial Information

  • Bank account details
  • UPI details
  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • Payment approval records

2.3 Operational Data

  • CDO and CRO records
  • Container numbers and validation data
  • Seal and transporter details (if applicable)
  • Approval and payment workflow records

2.4 Device & Authentication Data

  • Device information
  • Login logs and audit records
  • MPIN authentication data
  • Biometric authentication preference (fingerprint data is not stored by TCBA)

3. How We Use Information

  • Process container movement workflows
  • Validate container information
  • Facilitate secure payments and wallet transactions
  • Maintain ledger and reconciliation records
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized activity
  • Comply with GST, tax, and regulatory requirements
  • Maintain system security and audit logs

4. Data Sharing

We may share information only with:

  • Authorized YARD software systems via secure APIs
  • Payment gateway providers (e.g., RazorpayX)
  • Government or regulatory authorities when legally required

We do NOT:

  • Sell personal data
  • Share data for advertising purposes
  • Use stakeholder data for marketing without consent

5. Data Security

  • Encryption of financial and sensitive data
  • Secure API communication
  • Role-based access control
  • Fraud detection and monitoring systems
  • Duplicate transaction prevention mechanisms

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain active stakeholder accounts
  • Meet financial audit and reconciliation requirements
  • Comply with GST and tax regulations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Certain transaction records may be retained up to 8 years or as required by applicable Indian law.

7. User Rights

  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request account deactivation
  • Request transaction history
  • Request account deletion (subject to regulatory retention requirements)

8. Biometric Authentication

If enabled, fingerprint authentication is processed locally on the user’s device. TCBA does not collect, store, or transmit biometric fingerprint data.

9. Children's Privacy

The TCBA Mobile Application is intended for business users only and is not designed for individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

TCBA may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
